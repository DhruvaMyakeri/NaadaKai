# Surreal Road Collage Paper Burst

**One-liner:** A surreal digital collage of an asphalt road leading into a fan-like explosion of torn paper scraps, vintage maps, and fluffy clouds.

**Subject:** A central asphalt road with yellow dashed line recedes between two green grassy hills toward a stylized yellow sun or lightbulb icon at the horizon, from which torn paper strips and realistic clouds burst upward in a radial pattern.

**Visual details:** Portrait orientation. Mixed-media digital collage aesthetic. Bottom third is realistic road photograph. Upper two-thirds layered scraps including brown kraft paper, tan grid paper, blue crumpled paper, vintage maps, and pages with cursive and printed text. Realistic white fluffy clouds scattered overlapping paper elements. Top right has bold fragmented black-and-white typography. Natural greens and grays combined with earthy browns, bright blues, and yellow pops. Imaginative creative mood suggesting a journey or explosion of ideas.
