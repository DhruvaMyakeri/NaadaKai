# Surreal Skeletons Cosmic Portal Transition

**One-liner:** A psychedelic illustration of three connected skeletal figures transitioning from a monochromatic portal through a digital grid toward a vibrant rainbow gateway in space.

**Subject:** Three connected skeletal figures representing stages of evolution: a black-and-white skeleton emerging from a white oval portal on the left, holding hands with a blue-skinned skeletal figure in the center, who holds hands with a pink-skinned female skeleton stepping into a large circular rainbow portal on the right, pointing forward with a blue beam shooting from her eye.

**Visual details:** Landscape orientation. Black space filled with white stars. Green 3D wireframe grid forms floor and corner wall in middle-right. Figures have visible skeletal structures inside colored translucent bodies. Pink figure on right has fiery orange aura. Surrealist visionary art style with clean lines and vibrant high-contrast palette.

**Text in image:** MARCOS ALVARADO
