# Distorted City Street Oil Painting

**One-liner:** An expressionist oil painting of a narrow city street where buildings and road are dramatically warped into a surreal fish-eye curve.

**Subject:** A city street viewed from high angle with tall multi-story European-style buildings lining both sides; structures on the left curve sharply toward upper right. Several small cars drive on dark wet-looking asphalt below.

**Visual details:** Portrait orientation. Oil painting with visible thick textured brushstrokes and expressionistic feel. Palette of deep blues, muted yellows, ochre, and grays. Dark blue-black road with white lane markers. Extreme fish-eye lens distortion bending vertical architecture lines. Street curves away into distance. Upper right sky has pale blue and white paint swirled into Van Gogh-like vortex. Dark car in bottom center-left; street lamps and storefronts on sidewalks. Dazzling surreal slightly dizzying mood.

**Text in image:** Illegible stylized text on blue and red shop signs on ground floor
