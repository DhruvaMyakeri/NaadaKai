# Open Door Sunset Night Landscape

**Subject:** A standalone weathered blue wooden door, open to reveal a glowing sunset over distant hills, sitting in the middle of a vast dark meadow under a swirling night sky with a bright yellow celestial body in the upper left.

Night sky with single bright yellow moon or star amid swirling clouds. Foreground textured grass dotted with small red and blue wildflowers. Dark mountain silhouettes on horizon. Door centered creating surreal portal effect. Mystical, ethereal, dreamlike mood.
