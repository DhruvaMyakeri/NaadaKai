# Winding Road Forest Swirling Sky

**One-liner:** An impressionistic painting of a winding pale road through a dark evergreen forest under a massive hypnotic white and black swirling sky.

**Subject:** A light-colored winding dirt road snakes through a dense forest of dark silhouetted coniferous trees toward a sky dominated by a giant spiral vortex.

**Visual details:** Portrait orientation. Post-impressionist style with thick visible impasto brushstrokes giving heavy surface texture. Sky is dramatic vortex of cream-white and charcoal-black paint spiraling to central point. Dark evergreen trees with occasional warm orange and gold branch highlights. Pale textured road provides leading line from bottom foreground to middle ground. Moody atmospheric lighting with swirling sky as primary light source. Mystical expressive mood.
