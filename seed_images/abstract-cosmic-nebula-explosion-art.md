# Abstract Cosmic Nebula Explosion Art

**One-liner:** A vibrant stylized digital painting of a cosmic explosion or nebula radiating from a central bright point against a starry dark background.

**Subject:** A central burst of yellow-white light radiates outward into swirling textured clouds of teal and orange energy, resembling a supernova or nebula in deep space.

**Visual details:** Portrait orientation. Bright yellow-white light source at center with thick textured swirling lines of orange and teal radiating outward. Orange concentrated near center and at outer edges of teal sections. Teal areas have dense wavy fibrous texture. Background is near-black space with numerous small bright white dots and a few larger warm-toned circular spots suggesting stars and distant bodies. Psychedelic expressive style with heavy rhythmic brushwork. Dramatic lighting from central source illuminating surrounding gas clouds.
