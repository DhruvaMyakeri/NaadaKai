# Surreal Ocean Sunset Checkerboard Disco Ball

**One-liner:** A surrealist painting of an ocean sunset framed by harlequin-patterned pillars with a disco ball, planet, and checkerboard floor in foreground.

**Subject:** A calm ocean sunset viewed through two vertical pillars with blue and white harlequin diamond pattern. A mirrored disco ball and dark planet hang in a night sky transitioning to sunset. Foreground has black and white checkerboard floor with colored spheres and a potted plant.

**Visual details:** Portrait orientation. Airbrushed surrealism with 1980s aesthetic. Deep blues in upper sky and water contrast with bright orange-yellow setting sun and soft pink-orange clouds above horizon. Side walls repeat blue-white-black diamond harlequin pattern. Checkered ceiling with disco ball catching light glint. Dark planet suspended mid-air below disco ball. Potted plant with long green leaves and large colored spheres on checkerboard floor. Rainbow-colored bands cut diagonally across corners. Psychedelic dreamscape mood.
